Black Bean Veggie Burger Recipe

Home

Description

The following is a recipe for healthy black bean veggie burgers.

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 (16 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1/2 onion, cut into wedges
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon Thai chili sauce or hot sauce
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat. Lightly coat a sheet of aluminum foil with cooking spray.
  2. Mash black beans in a medium bowl with a fork until thick and pasty.
  3. Finely chop bell pepper, onion, and garlic in a food processor. Stir chopped vegetables into mashed beans.
  4. Combine egg, chili powder, cumin, and chili sauce in a small bowl; stir into mashed bean mixture to combine. Mix in bread crumbs until mixture is sticky and holds together.
  5. Divide mixture into four patties; place on the prepared foil.
  6. Grill on the preheated grill for about 8 minutes on each side.
  7. Serve hot.
