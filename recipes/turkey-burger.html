Turkey Burger Recipe

Home

Description

The following is a recipe for flavorful turkey burgers.

Ingredients

  • Turkey Patties
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Grill:Lightly oil and preheat the grill. Place turkey burgers on the grill, turning once, about 10 minutes.
  2. Oven:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Place the patties on the baking sheet and bake for about 15 minutes.
  3. Air fryer:Preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees F and place patties inside. Air fry for about 15 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  4. Add toppings and serve.
